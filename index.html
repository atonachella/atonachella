What are the benefits of digital audio workshops? (DAWs)


There are alot of misconceptions about digital music creation. You must still know all the same musical notation and theory that an analog insturment artist would have to know, along with being comfortable with digital components, and digital workspaces. The truth is that most musical projects end up being hybrids of both digital and analog, to create a final project. Both analog and digital production have their pros and cons, and the goal is to use the best of one creative tool, and avoiding or replacing the more laborious tasks with new approaches.


Digital equipment can capture analog creations
Editing content is streamlined and cost effective. Old forms of media caused mass amounts of expensive, physical waste.
Mixing and mastering can be done at home. You don't need a multi-million dollar studio OR exclusive industry access!!
